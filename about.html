Hi! I’m Valery, a graphic designer, trans woman, and major bookworm. I’m currently in my third year of studies in the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design. I uses the knowledge and experiences that I have gained from my studies and my daily life to create beautiful designs that are suited to each project’s specific needs. If you’d like to hire me for a project, or just talk ideas over coffee feel free to get in touch.
