About Me


I’m a graphic designer, photographer, and caffeine lover currently living in Toronto. I’m currently a full time student in the York University/Sheridan College Bachelor of Design Honours Program. I've loved art and design since I was little. I adore functional design and my work reflects that. When I'm not creating things you'll probably find me running or reading a book with a fresh cup of tea, earl gray, hot.
