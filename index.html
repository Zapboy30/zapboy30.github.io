About

I'm an aspiring graphic designer and caffeine lover in the Ottawa Valley.
I've loved art and good design since I was little. I love simple designs and my work reflects that.
When I'm not creating things you'll probably find me running or reading a book with a fresh cup of tea, earl grey, hot.
