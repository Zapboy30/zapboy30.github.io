Valery Marier is a graphic designer interested in the intersection of the physical and digital design worlds.

She combines her experiences with both physical and digital mediums and methods to create a multidiciplinary approach to her design practice. By doing this, she is able to create unique design solutions that are suited to each project’s specific needs and circumstances.
