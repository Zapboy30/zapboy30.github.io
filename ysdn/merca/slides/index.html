The current system of buying food is broken.

It’s nearly impossible for people to buy food in a completely sustainable way.

But this isn’t a result of laziness, it’s a result of complicated melange of interconnected systems that trap the majority of the population.

The solution? Introduce a new system.

Introduce a new normal.

Every once in a while a revolutionary product comes around that changes everything.

This is one of those.

Say hello to Mercä, our smart vending machine for the future.

Mercä combines the convienience of vending machines with the sustainability of byoc bulk food stores.

The problem with sustainable bulk food stores is that they are inconvienient.

Zero waste bulk food stores are few and far between and regular bulk food stores encourage the use of single use packaging.

Grocery stores are even worse, they're more convienient but have bad packaging practices.

There is simply no logical case where a single pepper needs to be packaged separately. © cbc Marketplace, April 2019.

And don’t even get me started on the current state of vending machines.

“Every year, Canadians throw away over 3 million tonnes of plastic waste. This represents up to $8 billion per year in lost value and wastes valuable resources and energy.”
–office of the prime minister

So how does Mercä solve this?

Our smart vending machine combines a combination of preexisting technologies and ideas into a sustainble and ultraconvienient food purchasing experience.

Say goodbye to forced plastic bags, blister packs, and containers. With Mercä you have to bring your own.

Plus getting food with Mercä is as easy as 1. 2. 3.

Pick → Place → Dispense
