The Project

For this project I redesigned the Wikipedia article for the Utah Tea Pot. The main goal of the Wikipedia article redesign was to be a lightweight and responsive website while still looking aesthetically pleasing. Keeping with the general design ethos of Wikipedia, I used a very minimal colour scheme consisting of two shades of blue, black, white, and gray.

Analysis & Preparation

Branding

Simple and sleek.

I used a fairly large sized Open Sans Regular — 18px on desktop — for my body text for ease of reading. The website’s word-mark is made up of two sizes of Halcyon Bold. I also added a pagination in the footer of each page for ease of use.
