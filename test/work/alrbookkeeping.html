ALR Bookkeeping ALR Bookkeeping is a small town bookkeeping company with big city charm.

ALR Bookkeeping Services is a bookkeeping company in Almonte, Ontario. When I took them on as a client they had no clear brand, save for a couple of business cards and a half finished landing page made in a drag ad drop website builder. My task was to give them a complete visual refresh that included a new logo, company letterhead, a new business card, and a fast and accessible website for their clients to access.

For the website I had to reinterpret the layout because having actual braille type on the event's website would be redundant; braille readers are able to interpret regular text digitally. I decided to keep the braille dots as a visual reference to the booklet, and I created a subtle accent animation with. Instead of utilizing different colours or underlines for accents and links, I opted instead to use motion to show text links as a nod to the tactile nature of the booklet.

"The concept is clean, bold, well thought out and multisensory. The rationale and detailed consideration around the typographical pairing and genre selection are on point, as the humanist style embodies many of the traits that make a typeface legible." Carolina Taylor RGD, UX Designer at the City of Mississauga
