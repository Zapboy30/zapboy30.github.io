The Project

I sought out to make an online ecommerce store. I created a simple, yet elegant design that people of all ages can use online. My aim was to build a website where the owners could sell a curated selection of their products Canada-wide, engage their users with their brand’s lifestyle & ideology, and persuade the website’s users into visiting the Comet’s brick and mortar location.
